Rich Moist Soothing Tencel Sheet Mask

Moisturize and Balance the Skin

Like it’s name, the Rich Moist Soothing Tencel Sheet Mask soothes sensitive, irritated, and inflamed skin. Containing an abundant amount of soothing, moisturizing, and skin-barrier repairing ingredients, the sheet mask is guaranteed to be a solution for those are experiencing dryness, redness, or irritation.

The Rich Moist Soothing Tencel Sheet mask contains Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice which is known to be an effective ingredient to soothe irritated sensitive skin. It is also packed full of various ceramides as well as centella asiatica to repair the skin’s barrier. This hypoallergenic sheet mask is made up of a double layered tencel gauze mask to ensure to fit into all face shape with comfort.

Features

  • Strengthen the skin barrier to areas with irritation
    The soothing sheet mask contains 5 types of ceramide, which are the constituents of the skin. This helps strengthen weakened skin barriers.
  • Mask full of moisturizing nutrients
    The Rich Moist Soothing Tencel Mask is formulated with Aloe Vera, Panthenol, Hyaluronic Acid and high concentration of various moisturizing ingredients to add energy and moisture to dry and exhausted skin.
  • Healing Gauze Sheet
    As the Healing Gauze sheet is doubled with 100% cotton and tencel fabric, it is designed to minimize skin irritation. The thin and flexible feature of the sheet mask allows it to comfortably adhere onto curved skin, and help the essence absorb well.

How to Use

Before opening, rub lightly for the sheet and liquid to mix well. After cleansing, place the sheet on clean/toned skin. Wait 15-20 minutes, and gently remove the mask. Tap lightly to let the essence fully absorb.
